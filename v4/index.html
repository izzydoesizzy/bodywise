What is Fertility Awareness? Work With Me

Hi! I'm Laura

I'm here for you, every step of the way

I know it's hard to figure out how to best take care of your sexual health, so I am here for you every step of the way.

Are you having side effects from birth control that you don't like? Do you have irregular periods, or wild pre-menstrual symptoms? Is your fertility a mystery to you?

I've been there. I have felt clueless and unsupported, not knowing my own body, and frustrated by the lack of options.

Then I found fertility awareness. Practicing the Fertility Awareness Method (FAM) has given me a deep understanding of my menstrual cycle, and the knowledge to support whole-body health. It is incredibly empowering. It's also the right of every person to know how their body works.

Learning how to do this was not difficult, and it only took a few cycles. As I gained confidence and charted consistently, I got much more than I bargained for.

It has helped me to:

Feel more connected to myself as I healed from trauma.

See the impact of allergies on my body.

Avoid pregnancy effectively.

Monitor the impact of using Plan B, getting vaccinations, and using different medications on my body.

Achieve pregnancy when I was ready.

Track the return of fertility as I breastfed, and continue avoiding pregnancy in the postpartum period.

Understand the cyclical nature of my mood, and much more!

I have been practicing FAM since 2014, and it has been nothing short of transformative.

I now feel much more empowered, confident, and at home in my own body. Importantly, it also allowed me to get off hormonal birth control, stop experiencing the negative impacts it was having on my mental health, and stop contributing to the harmful environmental impacts it can have.

Since I learned this for myself, it has been my passion to support others to gain this knowledge too.

Experience & Training

A decade of reproductive-health work

In the past 12 years, I've worked as a counsellor and manager with many leading Toronto organizations in sexual and reproductive health, abortion care, crisis counselling, and gender-based violence.

I also trained with Justisse College as a Holistic Reproductive Health Practitioner (HRHP) and Fertility Awareness Educator.

"Everyone deserves the opportunity to learn more about their body, and it is my life's work to support people who are ready to get to know themselves more."

My goal is to provide support that is unburdened by gender binaries. Everyone who is values-aligned is welcome to work with me, including non-binary people, genderqueer folks, the 2SLGBTQ+ community, and allied cis women.

If you have a menstrual cycle, you can learn to practice fertility awareness too.

Working with me is for you if...

You need an allied educator. You are sick of gender discourse in the menstrual health space.

You're ready to stop the pill, or other hormonal birth control that isn't working for you, and learn another effective option.

You're hormone-free right now. You are not currently taking gender-affirming hormone therapy, or hormonal birth control.

You want to conceive in the near future and prep your body for a healthy pregnancy.

You have fertility concerns and want to investigate what is going on, before paying for expensive fertility treatments.

You're willing to learn a new skill. It requires patience!
