Work With Me

Sexual & Reproductive Health Counselling & Practical Support

For more than 12 years, I have worked with leading Toronto organizations as a sexual and reproductive health counsellor. I am well-versed at working with people through some of the most difficult moments and decision-making of their lives.

I know that making decisions about our sexual lives, including navigating consent, deciding what to do about an untimely pregnancy, or choosing to leave an abusive partner, can feel incredibly isolating.

You deserve someone who is in your corner, ready to offer unconditional support.

I am here to lend a listening ear, share evidence-based information, and offer non-judgemental emotional and practical support. I work from a feminist peer support model, rooted in an anti-racism and anti-oppression framework.

Please note that while I am happy to speak with people remotely who are anywhere in the world, I can only support you with navigating healthcare services in Canada.

My counselling expertise includes:

  • Birth control options
  • Healthy relationships
  • Pregnancy options
  • Abortion support (counselling, accompaniment, practical support)
  • Living with a sexually transmitted infection (HPV, HSV, HIV)
  • Sexual violence (healing from sexual assault)
  • Family or intimate partner violence (including childhood trauma)
  • Crisis support
  • Suicide ideation and self-harm
  • Perinatal mental health (anxiety, depression, and other concerns during preconception, pregnancy, and postpartum)
  • Service navigation and referrals
